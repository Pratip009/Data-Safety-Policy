Account Deletion and Data Safety Policy

Introduction

At TS-Curo Hub, protecting your privacy and securing your data is paramount. This policy provides detailed information about how we handle your data, including the collection, usage, sharing, and protection of your personal information, as well as the process for deleting your account with us.

Data Collection and Use

We collect data to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, and Phone Number.
  • Usage Data: including how you use our app and interaction details.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is shared under limited circumstances, which include:

  • With your consent, for services you’ve requested.
  • For legal reasons, such as to meet any applicable law or regulatory requirements.

Data Security

We are dedicated to securing your personal information and employ various security measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account:

  1. Go to the Profile page within our app.
  2. Select “Delete Account” and send a mail to the admin on the given email address to delete and remove all your data.

If you want to delete your account along with all associated data, please contact the administrator directly at tapasi.samanta@gmail.com. Upon receiving your request, we will remove your data from our system with in 24 hours. Please note that this process may require verification of your identity to protect your privacy and security.

Note: Deleting your account will remove all data associated with it and cannot be undone.
